## What we look for in strong submissions

1. **MVP focus:** make the core workflow functional before adding extras
2. **Product sense:** sound judgment where the requirements leave room for interpretation
3. **Code quality:** clear structure, modular functions
4. **Reliability:** correct logic and test(s) for core behavior
5. **Reasoning:** clear rationale for key decisions and tradeoffs

*A complete, well-reasoned MVP with clean structure will score **higher** than a broad but shallow solution.*

*You may use AI tools, but submissions with excessive low-value AI-generated content will be declined.*
